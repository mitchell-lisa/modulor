## Role

**Title:** Co-Founder · Head of Finance (CFO track)

**Scope.** You own the money function end-to-end — not staff accounting, but
the financial discipline that turns a seed-stage entity into an investable,
operable, audit-ready company:

- Maintain the cap table and ownership ledger as shares issue, pool grants
  land, and the seed round closes
- Build and run the fundraise data room (historical spend, forward budget,
  cap table, 409A, governance docs) for investor diligence
- Own the budget-to-actuals discipline — $3M seed use-of-funds tracking,
  monthly burn reporting, runway forecasting
- Review term sheets, SAFE/priced-round mechanics, pro-rata provisions, and
  protective clauses on the company side of every investor conversation
- Coordinate the annual 409A valuation and any interim valuations triggered
  by priced rounds or material events
- Liaise with outside counsel, tax advisors, and the accounting firm once
  engaged; own the fiscal-year-end close
- Stand up the operational finance stack — Mercury/Brex account operations,
  QuickBooks, expense reimbursement policy, Ramp card program when ready
- Run investor reporting post-raise — monthly/quarterly updates, KPI
  dashboards, board financial pre-reads

**Commitment.** Part-time while you continue at UBS. We both expect that to
remain the structure through the seed round and early build; capacity ramps
toward full-time when the company can support the compensation step-down and
the role demands it.

co-founder roles. Specific reasoning for the 5% band:

   without a finance lead is the fastest way to sign a bad term sheet or
   blow out the cap table. You remove that risk.
2. Your UBS track record — 9 years, Analyst → Executive Director in Real
   Estate Finance — is a credibility signal on the team slide that
   investors read immediately. It changes how seriously our numbers get
   taken.
3. Your Wharton economics + entrepreneurship foundation plus a decade of
   institutional capital-markets work is the exact shape of experience a
   seed-to-Series-A CFO needs.
4. Family-trust premium — we're brothers. The alignment runs deeper than
   any comp structure could capture.

5% matches Luke's grant and puts the founding team on symmetric footing.
